## VISUAL DESIGN SYSTEM

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy/Dark Blue:** #003366 (backgrounds, headers, dominant color)
- **Secondary Orange/Gold:** #FF9500 (CTA buttons, accents, emphasis)
- **Accent Purple:** #7C3AED (secondary buttons, icons, highlights)
- **Text on Dark:** #FFFFFF (white)
- **Text on Light:** #1F2937 (dark gray)
- **Background Light:** #F3F4F6 (light gray)
- **Success Green:** #10B981 (checkmarks, positive indicators)
- **Alert Red:** #EF4444 (warnings, but use sparingly)

### Typography
- **Headings:** Modern sans-serif (Helvetica Neue, Inter, or similar), bold weight, 32-56px
- **Subheadings:** Same family, 24-28px, semi-bold
- **Body Text:** 16-18px, regular weight, line-height 1.6
- **CTA Text:** 16-18px, bold, uppercase or sentence case with emphasis
- **Small Text:** 12-14px, regular, medium gray

### Design Patterns
1. **Hero Section:** Dark navy background, white text, large headline, professional image (technician or home/business interior), orange CTA button
2. **Feature Cards:** White or light background, navy text, icon or small image, 3-4 cards per row, hover effects
3. **Trust Section:** Light background, grid of metrics (50+ Years | 24/7 Support | 45min Response | 100% Licensed)
4. **Testimonials:** Navy background, white text, customer photo, 3-5 testimonials in carousel or grid
5. **CTA Sections:** Alternating dark navy and light gray, prominent orange buttons, action-oriented copy
6. **Footer:** Dark navy, white text, logo, contact info, links

### Button Styles
- **Primary CTA:** Orange/Gold background (#FF9500), white text, 16-18px, bold, 12-16px padding, rounded corners (8px), hover: darker shade
- **Secondary CTA:** Purple background (#7C3AED), white text, same sizing
- **Tertiary:** Navy text on light background with border
- **All CTAs:** Must be clickable/actionable (phone link or form trigger)
